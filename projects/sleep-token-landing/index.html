Even In Arcadia

Sleep Token • 2025

Even in Arcadia is the fourth studio album by anonymous English rock band Sleep Token. The album was released on 9 May 2025 through RCA Records, to CD, LP, digital download, and streaming formats. The album was produced by Carl Bown and features writing credits from Sleep Token member Vessel. It was promoted by the release of three preceding singles; "Emergence" on 13 March 2025, "Caramel" on 4 April 2025, and "Damocles" on 25 April 2025. In the United Kingdom, the tracks peaked on the Official Charts Company's Official Singles Chart at respective numbers 17, 10, and 25, while on the Billboard Hot 100, they were positioned at numbers 57, 34, and 47. "Emergence" became their first charted track onto either of the two charts,[1] while "Caramel" became their first top-10 entry on the Official Singles Chart.[2]

Sundowning

Sleep Token • 2020

The group continued issuing songs from the album every two weeks up to the album release date. Sundowning received generally positive reviews from music critics. Kerrang! columnist Tom Shepherd gave the album a rating of four out of five, writing that it contains "moments here to truly savour, and ideas and experiences that feel unique", but noting that "the continuous nature of this dark mood entwined with the group's slow-burning, listless pace does begin to drag across 50-minute runtime"

Jaws Single

Sleep Token • 2018

Jaws is a cold-blooded assessment of a relationship defined by mutual consumption. Vessel rejects the role of a savior, choosing instead to lean into the toxicity of a partner who watches like a hunter. It is a song about the strange, sharp hunger that lingers when two people stop pretending to be kind.
